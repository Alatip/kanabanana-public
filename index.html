Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

KanaBanana (“the app”) is a hiragana and katakana learning app. Your privacy here is simple: the app does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data.

Data we collect

None. The app has no accounts, no sign‑in, and no analytics or tracking. The developer cannot see how you use the app.

Data stored on your device

Your learning data — practice progress, lesson results, and settings — is stored only on your device. It never leaves your device except through the optional iCloud backup below. You can erase it anytime via Settings → Reset, or by deleting the app.

iCloud backup (optional)

If you turn on iCloud backup, a copy of your progress is stored in your own iCloud account so you can restore it. This is handled by Apple under Apple's privacy policy; the developer has no access to it. You can disable it or remove the backup at any time.

Pronunciation

Optional spoken readings use your device's built‑in text‑to‑speech. This runs entirely on your device; nothing is sent to the developer or any third party.

Third parties

The app contains no third‑party advertising, analytics, or tracking, and shares no data with anyone.

Children

The app is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children.

Your rights

Because no personal data is collected, there is nothing for the developer to access, export, or delete. Data on your device stays under your control and can be deleted as described above.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.
